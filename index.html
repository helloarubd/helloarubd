The Style Guide

There are no rules, but here is how we love to style our pieces.

The Art of Layering

Start with a delicate choker, add a mid-length chain, and finish with a statement pendant. Mixing textures (like a snake chain with a cable chain) adds effortless depth.

Curated Stacks

Balance is key. Pair a bold, chunky ring on your index finger with two ultra-thin, minimalist bands on your ring finger for a look that feels intentionally undone.
