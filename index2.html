How to Wear It

There are no rules. Here's what we love.

01 — Layering

Depth over matching.

Start with a delicate choker, add a mid-length chain, finish with a statement pendant. Mixing textures — a snake chain against a cable chain — does more than matching metals ever could.

02 — Stacking

One statement, two whispers.

Balance is everything. Pair a bold ring on the index finger with two ultra-thin bands on the ring finger — a look that reads as intentional, not accidental.
